About us

Hi, we're Ash and Justin!

We've been together since we were 14, survived high school, adulthood, becoming parents and somehow still choose to spend all our time together.

We're a Sydney-based couple creating authentic UGC for brands that want content that feels real, relatable and genuinely engaging. Think less polished commercial, more "friend recommendation you actually trust."

Our content is inspired by our everyday life. Parenthood, routines, home life, travel, coffee runs, date nights and all the little moments in between.

Whether we're showcasing a product, sharing an experience or telling a story, our goal is simple: create content people actually want to watch.

And yes, there will probably be coffee involved!
